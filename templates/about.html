ABOUT US

When reading movie critics in the newspaper, what are the first thoughts that pop into your mind? Is it disagreement with the critic’s thoughts and perceptions about the particular movie or is it agreement? Hearing or reading someone’s criticism of a movie is a very sensitive process as it will most likely shape your experience of said movie in a way. If you’ve heard that the film is supposedly good, then you may perceive it differently. We believe the same goes for music and albums. Though rather than wanting to leave the reader with either the thought of agreement or disagreement, we want to leave them with an “awe” sensation because of how much time and effort we put into our reviews. In many ways, the work of a critic is easy. We risk very little yet enjoy a position over those who offer up their work and their selves to our judge.

OUR RATING PROCESS

When going through an album or a song, we hope your mind will strive to enjoy that album/song. The reason being is that music is made for enjoyment. If we cannot enjoy what we are listening to, then why listen to it at all? This is the thing that drives us here at mumble. When we are at work we listen to music, we rate it and categorize it so that you don’t have to. This is to make it easier for you to find that album or song which your mind will come to enjoy. When we first go to rate an album, we try our best to rate it fairly and without any bias. We rate it based on the amount of work which has gone into the lyrics, the song composition, how the songs fit each other and many other things. We also find our favorite song out of all the songs in the album. This is so that new listeners can have a taste of the album through that one song.
